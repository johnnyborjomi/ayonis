About Us

Nobody wants to pay for bureaucracy, but every time you work with a big company you come up against bureaucracy.

We all like microservices, because they are small, independent and fast. We don't like huge monolithic companies with a huge staffs you'd rather not have to pay. Are you sure that you want to pay for the new boss' secretary, or for the time they spend creating reports on other reports?
This is something we definitely do not like or want to be. So we created a new kind of company. It is a family-style company. We are a group of old friends who have known each other for ages, all of whom love coding. We are a microservices company. We don't need to make all the money in the world.

We just want to do our job the best that we can and bring results to our customers.

- Medical Software Development
