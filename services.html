Quality Assurance

How much is quality worth? When a user has to deal with annoying problems, the original value of the product is lost. Identifying all potential problems and usability issues can be a tough and time-consuming task. All products Aionys delivers are thoroughly tested by our Quality Assurance engineers.
Aionys offers various QA consulting services to our clients, such as:

• Manual testing

Testing is the key to identifying usability issues. QA engineers act as end users and check to make sure all features of the application work correctly and in full accordance with the requirement specifications. We provide the talent and tools to deliver your product with a faster time-to-market as well as measurable improvements in quality and cost reduction. Leveraging our services allows you to test earlier and more often and to identify defects when they are less costly to remediate.

• Automated testing

Numerous repetitions of identical test cases are a waste of time and money. Automated tests may be written once and then can be run quickly and frequently after. This keeps projects cost-effective and guarantees a long maintenance life. That's why we are the preeminent provider of test automation, combining the speed and cost-savings benefits of software test automation with low-cost offshore testing resources.
